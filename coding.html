About Coding at The Dragon’s Mind

Here, coding is seen as both a craft and a creative process. The Dragon’s Mind encourages learning, experimentation, and the blending of different programming paradigms and technologies—never rigid dogma, always exploration.

  • Development Practices
    Agile, DevOps, version control, and other methodologies that shape how we build and maintain software.
  • Resources
    Tutorials, documentation, open source projects, and community forums to support your coding journey.
  • Creativity in Code
    Exploring generative art, creative coding, and the intersection of logic and imagination.
