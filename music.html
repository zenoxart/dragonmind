About Music at The Dragon’s Mind

Music here is seen as a bridge between emotion, mathematics, and creativity. The Dragon’s Mind encourages you to explore, experiment, and discover new perspectives—never rigid rules, always inspiration.

Music Theory Essentials

  • Scales & Modes
    The foundation of melody and harmony. Explore major, minor, pentatonic, and modal scales to understand how music moves and feels.
  • Chords & Harmony
    Learn how chords are built, how they relate, and how progressions create emotion and tension in music.
  • Rhythm & Meter
    The heartbeat of music. Discover time signatures, syncopation, polyrhythms, and the role of rhythm in different styles.
  • Intervals
    The distance between notes shapes melodies and harmonies. Recognize and use intervals to enrich your musical vocabulary.
  • Notation & Ear Training
    Reading, writing, and listening—develop your ability to understand music on paper and by ear for deeper creativity and communication.
  • Timbre & Dynamics
    Explore the color and character of sound, and how dynamics shape musical expression and storytelling.
